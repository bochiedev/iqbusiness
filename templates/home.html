We Are.

We at IQ Business Training & Consulting are a family whose only purpose is to be your trusted go to for all matters capacity building and corporate training. Based in Nairobi, Kenya we've enjoyed a regional presence initially as a consulting partner later diversifying our expertise into the vast world of corporate development.

Our vision is borne by the continued and rapid growth of the African workspace and the development of international best practices towards workplace wellness.

With the diverse nature of our clients and partners industrial scope, our portfolio touches on; Health and Safety, Oil, Gas and Renewable Energy, Leadership and Organizational Development, Business operations, Strategic Human Resource, Logistics and Supply Chain Management, Healthcare, Banking, Finance and Information Technology.

IQ Business Training & Consulting places skills improvement and career advancement training at the heart of its cradle, we’re endeared with the crucial mission of putting the client first as they achieve excellence, quality & innovation.
